# 📈 End-to-End Sales Forecasting & Demand Intelligence System

A Data Science project developed during the **Week 3 & Week 4 Internship** to analyze historical retail sales, forecast future demand, detect anomalies, segment products based on demand behavior, and provide an interactive business dashboard.

## 🚀 Live Demo

* **Streamlit App:** https://salesforecasting-prabhu0712.streamlit.app/
* **Google Colab Notebook:** https://colab.research.google.com/drive/19pf1xlMEbBqAlajWyfyzYzW67G__3KYS?usp=sharing

---

## ✨ Features

* Sales Data Analysis & EDA
* Time Series Decomposition
* Sales Forecasting using:

  * SARIMA
  * Facebook Prophet
  * XGBoost
* Product & Region-wise Forecasting
* Anomaly Detection (Isolation Forest & Z-Score)
* Product Demand Segmentation (K-Means & PCA)
* Interactive Streamlit Dashboard

